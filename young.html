THE FIRST 14 YEARS

All of my most memorable books, shows, and movies from childhood to the end of middle school.

Avatar: The Last Airbender

This is the very first tv series I have ever finished, and it is also probably one of the best series ever made. There is not one boring episode, and the overall plot is so well done. There is no show that gets me as excited as this one.

The Conjuring

This was the horror movie to start my middle school tradition of having horror movie sleepovers with my friends. I will never forget about the dresser scene, and hide and clap will forever creep me out. This is one of the horror movies that I will rewatch over and over again for the nostalgia.

Encyclopedia Brown

These are a series of short detective stories that I really enjoyed when I was a kid. There's really only one story I remember, and I really only remember the 'gotcha' moment of that story, but for some reason it has stuck in my brain since 4th grade. This book taught me that if someone is filing their nails after claiming to take an hour long bath, they're lying about the bath because their nails would be so soft the file would shred them.

Frozen

Frozen changed the game for movie soundtracks for me. I had the Youtube video for "Let it Go: Multilingual Version" playing on repeat for months after, and I still queue half of the songs when I go to karaoke.
